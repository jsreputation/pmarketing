FAQs

MEMBERSHIP:

What is PLUS! Rewards Digital Program?

PLUS! Rewards Digital is an upgraded loyalty program that rewards shoppers everytime they buy in Abenson, Electroworld, Waltermart Supermarket, W Department Store, abensonHOME, Homeplus, SB Furniture, and Habitat.

Who can apply for a PLUS! Rewards Digital account?

Applicant must be above 18 years old and a non-corporate account customer. Existing PLUS! Card holder can also apply and link their accounts by signing up at www.plus.com.ph and filling out the PLUS! Card no. field.

How long does my membership last?

Your PLUS! Rewards Digital account will be valid for a period of two (2) years, given that a record purchase is made within the said duration.

POINTS EARNING:

How do I earn points?

Present your PLUS! Rewards digital card to the cashier before any purchase transaction at any participating establishments. Each P500 purchase equals 10 points or P1 ECash.

I spent less than the minimum required amount of P500, do I earn points?

Yes you will still earn points but at the rounded down value. Eg. You purchase a P499 transaction, the 9.98 points is rounded down to 9 points or P0.90 ECash.

If I am an existing PLUS! Card holder, can I still use my points earned from my previous membership?

Yes, previous points earned from the old PLUS! Program will be credited to the new PLUS! Rewards Digital Account once you sign up to www.plus.com.ph and fills out the PLUS! Card no. field.

I don’t have an internet connection, can I still earn points?

Yes you can still earn points by keeping a screenshot of your digital account. You may present your digital account barcode to the cashier before any purchase transaction.

POINTS REDEMPTION:

How can I use my points to pay for my purchase?

PLUS! Rewards points has an equivalent ECash value that can be used to pay for any purchase at participating establishments. A minimum balance of P100 ECash is needed to start redeeming.

When can I start using my points for redemption?

You may start redeeming your ECash once it has a balance of P100. You may pay any amount purchased using your ECash balance.

Can other people redeem my points?

No, in order to ensure the validity of redemptions, other people can not redeem your points.

CARD SECURITY & PIN:

How do I change my pin code?

Under the “MY ACCOUNT” Tab, choose the “Change Pin Code” Option. You will be asked for the new pin code and a One Time Pin will be sent to you registered number.

What do I do if I forget my pin code?

Click on the “Forgot Pin” found on the sign up page. A prompt to enter your mobile number will appear. An OTP will be sent to the registered mobile number to reset the password.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1) I deleted the SMS notification containing my Player Code and the link to the Game Site. I cannot log-in. What should I do?

Please call the HSBC Customer Service Hotline at (02) 85-800 to request for the re-sending of the SMS notification containing the Player Code and link to the Game Site.

2) I do not have a smartphone. Will I be able to access in my desktop or laptop?

Yes. The HSBC Game Site may be accessed using a laptop or desktop. Please type in the URL link sent to you via SMS / eDM in your internet browser. You must have an internet connection or mobile data to be able to access the Game Site.

4) Will my Player Code change every cut-off date?

No. You will have the same Player Code which you need to enter every time you log-in to the HSBC Game Site.

5) The number of stickers in my account were not updated.

Please note that a minimum single-receipt straight purchase of Php1,000 entitles you to earn 1 puzzle piece while a minimum single-receipt installment transaction of Php3,000 entitles you to earn 2 puzzle pieces. These are updated based on the cut-off schedule. Kindly refer to your Statement of Account to validate your transactions. Please call the HSBC Customer Service Hotline at (02) 85-800 to report if there are still concerns.

6) Do I need to redeem my Instant Reward during the same day I played?

You have the option to redeem at a later time. Your unclaimed rewards and Sticker Card is found in your profile at the HSBC Game Site. Redemption Period is until December 11, 2019 for all earned Instant Rewards/Prizes. Instant Rewards left unclaimed will be forfeited after the Redemption Period while cardholders who have not logged in and used up their chances to play will be forfeited by October 15, 2019.

7) Merchant is not aware of the promo. They are not allowing me to redeem. What do I do?

The merchant partner is trained to accept qualified redemptions. Please bring the redemption requirements as indicated in the Promo Mechanics. Kindly call our hotline at 85-800 for any other concerns.

HSBC Credit Cards
Shop & Collect Christmas Game 2019 Promo

  1. I deleted the SMS notification containing my Player Code and the link to the Game Site. cannot log-in. What should I do?

    Please call the HSBC Customer Service Hotline at (02) 8858-0000 to request for resending of the SMS notification containing the unique player code and link to the game site.

  2. I do not have a smartphone. Will I be able to access in my desktop or laptop?

    Yes. The HSBC game site may be accessed using a laptop or desktop. Please type in the URL link sent to you via SMS / eDM in your internet browser. You must have an internet connection or mobile data to be able to access the Game Site.

  4. Will my unique player code change every cut-off date?

    No. You will have the same Player Code which you need to enter every time you log-in to the HSBC Game Site.

  5. My chances to earn stamps were not updated. I was supposed to receive more.

    Please note that a minimum single-receipt straight purchase of PHP3,000 earns you 1 stamp while a PHP5,000 single-receipt installment transaction earns you 3 stamps. These are updated based on the cut-off schedule. Kindly refer to your Statement of Account to validate your transactions. Please call the HSBC Customer Service Hotline at (02) 8858-0000 to report if there are still concerns.

  6. Do I need to redeem my reward/s during the same day I played?

    You have the option to redeem at a later time. You have until April 30, 2020 to use up your chances to earn stamps to get your rewards. Unclaimed stamps and rewards in the game site shall be forfeited after the said date.

  7. Merchant is a not aware of the promo. They are not allowing me to redeem. What do I do?

    All merchant partners are trained to accept qualified redemptions. Please bring the redemption requirements as indicated in the Promo Mechanics. Kindly call our hotline at (02) 8858-0000 for any other concerns.

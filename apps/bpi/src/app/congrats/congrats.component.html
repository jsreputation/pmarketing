Here's how to receive your Netflix rebate
  • Make sure that your BPI Credit Card must be the card used as payment method in your Netflix account
  • The Netflix subscription fee must be reflected in your statement of account
  • You will receive a notification from BPI once the Netlix rebate has been credited to your account.
  • You will see the Netflix rebate credited to your account in your next statement of account.
  • Only one Netflix rebate will be credited each month.

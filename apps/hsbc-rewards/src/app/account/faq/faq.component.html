HOW TO REDEEM YOUR REWARDS
  1. Log in to <insert URL> using your player code* and the last 4 digits for your HSBC Credit Card number.
  2. View the Rewards Catalogue specific to your HSBC Credit Card.
  3. Click on the item you wish to redeem and choose the following, as applicable:
    1. Quantity of items you wish to redeem
    2. Redemption Center of items for pick-up
    3. Pure Points or Fast Track** redemption

      4. Click on the submit button.

  5. Claim your Reward items
    1. Items for pick-up
      1. A unique claim code shall be sent to your mobile number in HSBC records.
      2. Once received, proceed to the redemption center and present the following:
        • SMS indicating the claim code
        • HSBC Credit Card used for redemption
        • Valid government-issued ID
    2. For Lazada e-Wallet credits
      1. To receive your HSBC Rewards, you must activate your Lazada E-Wallet.
      2. An SMS notification shall be sent to your mobile number in HSBC records once E-Wallet credits has been credited to your account.
      3. Once received, you can now shop using your Lazada E-Wallet Credits.
    3. For Zalora e-Gift Code
      1. An SMS notification shall be sent to your mobile number in HSBC records containing a unique E-Gift code.
      2. Once received, you can avail of the GC code upon checkout or via Wallet Credit.
    4. For miles redemption
      • Cardholders who wish to redeem miles must first call HSBC hotline to enroll in the Mileage Program.
      • An SMS notification shall be sent to your mobile number in HSBC records once redemption has been processed.
* Each HSBC Credit Card has a unique player code
** Under the Fast Track redemption, Cardholders will be eligible to claim the Rewards at lower Bonus Points and charge the cash portion to his/her HSBC Credit Card. The use of the HSBC Credit Card to pay the cash portion under Fast Track redemption will generate additional Bonus Points for the Cardholder.
HOW TO USE YOUR LAZADA E-WALLET
  1. To receive your HSBC Rewards, you must activate your Lazada E-Wallet
    1. Log in to your Lazada account via https://www.lazada.com.ph or the Lazada mobile app and click on “Manage My Account”
    2. On the left hand side select “Lazada E-Wallet”, and click “Verify my account”
    3. Click on “Verify my email” once you receive the email confirmation

      Note: We will use your email address as indicated in HSBC records. Your Lazada E-Wallet credits will be credited to this email address.

    4. You will then be prompted to verify your mobile number. Fill in your mobile number and key in the OTP number that you will receive via SMS
    5. Fill in your personal info and click “Go to my wallet”. Wallet is completely activated.
  2. To shop using Lazada E-Wallet Credits
    1. Log in to your Lazada account via https://www.lazada.com.ph or the Lazada mobile app.
    2. Select item/s you wish to purchase and click “add items to cart”.
    3. Click “Proceed to Checkout”.
    4. Enter shipping and billing details then click “Proceed to Pay”.
    5. Select “Lazada E-Wallet” as payment
HOW TO USE YOUR UNIQUE GIFT CARD CODE ON ZALORA
  1. How to avail of GC code upon checkout:
    1. Log-on to www.zalora.com.ph
    2. Click on Shop Women or Shop Men to begin.
    3. Select product categories.
    4. Select product.
    5. Select size and click on "Add to Bag".
    6. Once finished, click CHECKOUT NOW to proceed.
    7. Log in or Register by Signing in via Facebook. Input Customer Information such as name, phone number and delivery address.
    8. Select "Credit/Debit Card" as Payment Method and add your HSBC credit card information.
    9. Click "Have a promo code? Enter it here".
    10. Enter your Unique Gift Card code then click APPLY
    11. Click "Order Now" to finalize order
    12. Discount will be deducted from subtotal.
    13. Click ORDER NOW to finalize the order.
  2. How to avail of GC code via Wallet Credit (Desktop):
    1. Log-on to www.zalora.com.ph
    2. Hover your mouse on the Account icon and a drop down will appear then go to 'My Wallet'
    3. Enter your unique Gift Card Code under "Have a Gift Card? Add it to your wallet" and click "Add Credit"
    4. The value of the Gift Card will appear in 'My Store Credit'
    5. Enjoy shopping!
  3. How to avail of GC code via Wallet Credit (Mobile App):
    1. Open the ZALORA app
    2. Log-on to your account
    3. Go to 'MENU' then go to 'Wallet Credit'
    4. Enter your Unique Gift Card code and click "Apply".
    5. The value of the Gift Card will appear in 'My Store Credit'
    6. Enjoy shopping!
TERMS AND CONDITIONS
  1. ZALORA GC codes are not for sale and cannot be exchanged for cash.
  2. Once the ZALORA GC code is added to your ZALORA Wallet as Wallet Credit, this will not expire.
  3. ZALORA Wallet credit will automatically be deducted upon purchasing on ZALORA.
